About the Event

Come Out & Show Your Support

Microsoft is proud to sponsor the NWA Catfish, Corndogs & Cornhole event benefiting Mercy Hospital Northwest Arkansas. This is your chance to gather with colleagues, bring the family, and represent our brand in the community.

The event is completely free for Microsoft employees and their families — fully funded by Microsoft. Just show up anytime between 8 AM and 2 PM and enjoy the day. No registration required.

Look for the Microsoft tent and banner — that's your home base. Grab a chair, relax in the shade, and enjoy everything the day has to offer.

Come out and show your support for our brand in support of NWA Mercy Hospital.

— Vic Miles · RCG Industry Team · Microsoft
